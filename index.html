Who We Are

About Us

GLOBAL PRIME ORBIT is an Import–Export and Trading Company dedicated to delivering quality products worldwide. Our portfolio spans across agro commodities, clothing such as traditional handloom sarees, handmade jewellery, unique handicrafts, and elegant home décor.

We specialize in fresh organic vegetables, fruits, spices, nuts, soft drinks, and premium textiles and fabrics. Our mission is to flourish together with global trade by building strong partnerships and maintaining the highest standards of quality in every transaction.
